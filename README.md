# Smart Grocery Assistant – Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

Smart Grocery Assistant does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All information you enter into the app — such as pantry items, shopping lists, and expiration dates — remains on your device and is never transmitted to any server.

## Information We Collect
We do not collect any personal information.  
We do not use analytics, tracking tools, or third‑party data services.

## Data Storage
All data is stored locally on your device.  
You may delete the app at any time to remove all stored data.

## Third‑Party Services
Smart Grocery Assistant does not integrate with any third‑party services that collect user data.

## Children’s Privacy
Smart Grocery Assistant does not collect personal information from children or adults.
